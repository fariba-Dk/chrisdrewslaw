About


Technology, New Media, Entertainment and Product Attorney

Chris Drews is a seasoned Technology, Entertainment, New Media and Product attorney with over 25 years experience practicing in Los Angeles, California. Chris spent nearly 20 of those years with The Walt Disney Company where he served as Deputy Chief Counsel overseeing Business and Legal Affairs for Disney’s interactive, new media, video game and mobile segments.

Chris opened his boutique Santa Monica office in 2020 to assist technology and media companies large and small navigate complex commercial transactions, and to serve as outside General Counsel to burgeoning enterprises. Having served on the client side of the profession for most of his career, Chris uniquely understands the legal needs and budgetary pressures facing companies at any scale Accordingly, Chris brings a very practical, business-first approach to his legal services by cutting through much of the unnecessary bureaucracy over-lawyering that frequently burdens complex transactions. Delivering superior legal services with practicality and cost efficiency is Chris’s sole objective. If you or your company has any technology or commercial legal needs, please do not hesitate to reach out. We would be happy to help!
